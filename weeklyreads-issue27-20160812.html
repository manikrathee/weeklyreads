WeeklyReads: Issue 27

WeeklyReads is an email list for design, development and much more. Brought to you by a human on the internet.
 



Inspiration and more


VSCO Open Studio
VSCO created a studio space in NY for photographers to create and collaborate.


Cheers, by H&Co
A page that shows various H&Co type combinations on wine labels. Go exploring!


Practical SVG, A Book Apart
Chris Coyier, the wonderful person behind CSS Tricks, wrote a book with everything you need to know about using SVGs on the web. A must-read.




Design and Development


Framer for iOS
Framer now has an official iOS client so you can preview prototypes directly on your devices.


Improved Sharing Framer.js
Framer just launched some amazing updates prototype sharing. You can now set metadata, add guided interactions and more.


Lessons Learned: Using a static site generator at scale
This article is full of insight that can help inform your decisions before you get in too deep. Static Site Generators are great, but they have their pitfalls.


Code.gov
An open source project, from and for the US Government, to help change how tech is used.


The problem with breadcrumb trails
A dense but englightening read on the details of creating breadcrumbs and what they mean to your users.


Sketch Focus, a plugin
Sketch Focus brings to-do's right into Sketch so you never forget to make that last-minute change. You can even link layers to your to-do's.


The Essential Meta Tags for Social Media
A comprehensive guide to social media tags, brought to you by CSS Tricks.


Medium: Sketch Resizing Cheat Sheet
Sketch 39 introduced some huge improvements over resizing elements and groups. Peter Nowell walks us through those changes and the new features designers have at their disposal.


Unused CSS
A tool that explores your CSS codebase and gives you a breakdown of unused code. If you upgrade to premium, they'll even fix your css files and let you download them. (Full disclosure: I haven't used the paid features because I never trust apps to modify large, complex css files.)




Apps


Notion: A unified workspace for modern teams.
A web app that brings docs, wikis, sprints and tasks together. The marketing page is really well done, so even if you don't have a need for this, check it out.


Purple, for iOS
A Facebook Messenger bot that delivers interactive election updates.


Twitter Moments are opening up!
Twitter Moments have been opened up to more creators and it will soon be available for everyone.


Exist for iOS, Android and Web
Exist is an app that works with other services and helps gather data and deliver insights about your daily activity.
