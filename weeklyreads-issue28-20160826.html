Design and Development


Canvas for iOS is out
The great team at Canvas have just released their iOS app so now you can work on your projects from anywhere, with anyone.

A piece on Medium that walks us through the principles behind Pinterest's redesign. Get your highlighter ready for this one.


TypeTerms
An animated guide to typography terminology.


Principle 2.0
Principle, the prototyping tool, just hit 2.0 and announced "Components" to help you build smarter.


Sketch Symbol Best practices
This Medium piece has some great tips. Many of them are common sense for experience designers, but it is worth a read anyway.


rel=noopener
A good write-up on the dangers of allowing 'target="_blank"' on links, especially with user-generated content. Definitely worth a read.


Hidden Gems of Sketch
Learn how to collapse all artbaords at once, use custom shortcuts and more.


Designing better data tables
When you start working with datasets, you'll thank your past self for reading this. If you already work with datasets, you will probably be familiar with most of these.
