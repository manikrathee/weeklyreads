WeeklyReads: Issue 24

WeeklyReads is an email list for design, development and much more. Brought to you by a human on the internet.
 


Last weekend, I visited the PACE gallery with my wife and snapped a few photos - if you're in the Bay Area, I highly suggest making a visit.



Lingo App, by Noun Project
"The best way to organize, share and use all your visual assets in one place – all on your desktop." - I have been beta testing this for a few weeks and I really love their approach. This is well worth a try.


Tracking and Changing animations in Chrome DevTools
This new feature, described by @umaar, is incredible. If you work with CSS animations on web, you need to check it out.


Craft 2.0 Preview
Craft, InVision's Sketch plugin, is about to get more powerful with the Silver Flowers acquisition (see below)


Invision acquires Silver Flows
Invision, the company that makes prototyping tools for designers, has acquired Silver Flows, the startup that helped designers prototype directly within Sketch. Invision are positioning themselves to change web design in the way Adobe did a while ago. I will be eagerly watching Invision's releases.


Modern Layouts, Video
@JenSimmons, host of The Web Ahead podcast, walks us through some pretty neat web layouts inspired by print.


Adobe XD is now available
Adobe's answer to Sketch and prototyping has arrived.


Typeface for Mac
"Your favorite font app" - I haven't used this yet, but I trust Khoi's opinion.


Lumi: Ash Huang
Lumi interviewed Ash Huang about her book, The Firesteel and more. You should read it — She's great.


Unfiltered.news
This is a really cool visualization to see what news is popular by regions across the world.
